Introduction to Chalcopyrite

Chalcopyrite is the most important copper raw materials, mostly appear as brass, the surface will often have blue, purple brown and other different colors, and accompanied by metallic luster, its hardness, mineral processing is relatively simple. In China's Jiangxi, Gansu, Tibet, Shanxi and other provinces have a large number of high-quality resources distribution.

Chalcopyrite ore dressing more use of flotation process, in order to achieve comprehensive utilization of ore to improve the economic efficiency of enterprises, Red Star machine manufacturers devote themselves to the study, designed the most perfect mineral processing lines, and provide all the ancillary equipment. The launch of the production line, has been highly recognized by users, then what is its production line equipment? What is the process flow? What are the advantages of the production line? The following will be introduced for everyone.

Brass ore dressing production line equipment

The equipment used in the brass ore dressing production line includes jaw crusher, ball mill, flotation machine, dryer, screw classifier and other major equipment. In addition, there are vibration feeder, circular vibrating screen, belt conveyor and its related Auxiliary equipment and so on.

1, jaw crusher: jaw crusher is the main purpose of large chalcopyrite for the initial broken, its structure is simple, small size, broken than large, high efficiency, strong quality, and the price is very cheap and affordable, is yellow Copper mine broken the best choice;

2, ball mill: ball mill is broken after the chalcopyrite particles continue to deep processing grinding, the grinding efficiency, yield, good effect, and the equipment itself, energy-saving capacity, a single device can save 5-10 million Electricity is a good chalcopyrite grinding equipment;

3, flotation machine: flotation machine is the role of extraction of copper concentrate, the use of advanced technology production, with a high degree of automation, low investment costs, energy saving, efficient environmental protection, long life and other excellent quality, is the whole chalcopyrite Mineral processing line of key equipment;

4, the dryer: the dryer is the flotation of copper concentrate for dehydration, the price is very economical, cheaper than other drying equipment on the market 5-10 million, and drying capacity, the highest Can reach 60.8t-76t per hour, the use of the effect is extremely significant;

5, the spiral classifier: Spiral grading machine is mainly after the grinding of chalcopyrite powder sieving, washing, the manufacturing process superb, high-end material selection, so the quality is more reliable, more excellent performance, the sort of copper Fine powder fine uniform, excellent quality.

Chalcopyrite Mineral Processing Process

The large chalcopyrite mined is first sent by the vibrating feeder to the jaw crusher, and then the ball mill is fed evenly under the action of the belt conveyor. The treated material is subjected to the classification of the spiral classifier , The appropriate material can enter the flotation stage, do not meet the requirements of the copper ore powder will be sent to the ball mill again; grinding out the qualified copper powder was sent to the flotation machine, while adding appropriate amount of the corresponding agent with water, Mineral powder mixing thoroughly to achieve the separation effect; Finally, dry by the dryer can be dehydrated.
