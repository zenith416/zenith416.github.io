Zenith Portable Limestone Crushing Line

Project Background:
Location: Riyadh, Saudi Arabia (20 minutes by car from Riyadh city)
Raw Material: limestone
Feed Size: 500mm
Output Size Requirement: 0-3/16’’, 3/16’’-3/8’’, 3/8’’-3/4
Capacity: about 70-80 cubic meter per hour
Working hours: 15 hours per day
Installation Time: Late 2011
 
Customer Feedback:
“We haven’t replaced any spare part after continuous running for 16 months. Everything is still in perfect condition so far!”
