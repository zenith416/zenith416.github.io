Calcined dolomite production line

Dolomite beneficiation equipment

Dolomite beneficiation equipments consist of jaw crusher, inflatable flotation machine, mine ball mill, dry magnetic separator and spiral classifier, etc.

Dolomite beneficiation process

Dolomite contains many impurities. In dolomite beneficiation process, heavy media separation and flotation will be used to process dolomite. For example, Kaiser aluminum chemical company in California, America, can process the dolomite ores that contain quartz, serpentine and granite. The technological process contains crushing, ore washing, sieving and heavy medium separation, etc. Heavy medium separation uses the Wemco sorting cone, separation density is 2.65~2.75g/cm3 and the size of selected ores is +6mm. The floating ores are regarded as waste products, the same with the ores which come from two previous sieving paragraphs and whose grain size is 12 mm and 6 mm. The sinking ores come through the following crushing and sieving work then are processed into finished ores.

The ore process flow uses the principle that is crushing- ore washing-sieving. According to the different uses, the products in corresponding grain level will be produced. For example, in dolomite mine in Nanjing, China, the mined ores, whose MgO content is 20% or more and SiO2 content is below 2%, reach the industry standard and first-grade product index. What's more, the ores are quite pure so that the ore washing work is not needed.

The raw ores come through jaw crusher for coarse crushing, bar screen for pre-screening. After the medium crushing by cone crusher, the products come through the first sieving and are classified into three grades, that are -20mm,-50+20mm and +50mm. The products that are in -50+20mm level and +50mm level will be merged then come through the second sieving to get the products whose grain size is 10~30mm、25~50mm or 40~80mm. And the ore grains in -20mm level come through the third sieving to get the products whose grain size is 0~5mm、5~13mm、10~20mm. Among the six kinds of products, the ores in 50~25mm level and 30~10mm level are used as dolomites which are used for smelting. The ores in 0~5mm are used for sintering. The ores in 80~40mm are provided to the users who have special demands. Other ore products are used as building stone. The dolomite ores that contains many impurities can be processed by heavy media separation and flotation.

The application of impact crusher in dolomite

Previously, many dolomite ore production lines have used hammer crusher. Although the hammer crusher has a certain of advantages (small volume and large treatment capacity), the impact crusher looks better than the impact crusher because their working principles are not same.

Working process of impact crusher is free crushing stage. When the materials come into the chamber firstly, the materials are struck by hammer in a high speed and the materials will strike each other. This is a virtuous circle process, which plays a role that is using power to hit power. The circle process will be repeated for many times until the materials are crushed into a certain particle size. So the reduction ratio of impact crusher is larger and the impact crusher has higher energy utilization, which has more advantages compared with hammer. So the impact crusher is often used for coarse crushing, medium crushing and fine crushing brittle materials with low or medium hardness, such as coal, quartz, dolomite, calcium carbide, lime stone and gypsum.

Dolomite powder grinding

Dolomite powder grinding machine uses this process flow: the large blocks of materials are crushed into small ones by crusher, then the materials are sent into storage silo by elevator. Then through vibrating feeder and slant feed pipe, the materials are sent to dispersing materials plate, which is on the upper of the dial.

The materials are going to circle around by centrifugal force and come into raceway of the grinding ring. Then the materials are stroke, rolled, ground by ring roller. Through processed by three ring roads, the materials become powders. Through the suction action, the high pressure positive blower absorbs the outside air into the blower and the crushed materials are also absorbed into the powder concentrator.

In powder concentrator, the rotating impeller make coarse materials back for re-grinding. The fine powders that are suitable for demands come into whirlwind powder collector with air flow. At last, the fine powders are discharged by discharging valve on the bottom and become the final products. The airflow with little fine powder will cleaned by pulse dust collector and then the airflow are discharged by fan and silencer.
