Aggregate Processing Plant

In aggregate production process, many different types of aggregate processing plant involved. The production can be classified into crushing, screening, washing, sand making etc.

The aggregate crushing plant commonly used includes Jaw Crusher, Cone Crusher and Impact Crusher. Jaw crusher: Primary crushing. For coarse concrete aggregate production. Impact crusher: secondary crushing, high reduction ratio, good cubical shape, for ready mixed concrete aggregate and manufacturing sand production. Cone crusher: Tertiary crushing, fine crushing; high capacity and yield, for concrete aggregate and sand making in large scale. VSI crusher: Fine crushing, superior cubically shaped product, lower wear costs, for sand making and fine grain powder in cubical shape production.

Screening plant: In order to be graded, the gathered natural raw materials or the crushed mixture should be screened. There are two kinds of grading methods, hydraulic screening and mechanical screening. The large scale screening usually uses mechanical screening. There are two kinds of mechanical screening, eccentric vibration and inertia vibration.

Washing plant: sand washing plant is an important machine in aggregate processing, it helps wash out the impurity material and produce high quality clean aggregates.
